Black Cherry Plum

Prunus cerasifera nigra

Burgundy leaves and grey bark that fissures with age. Pink blossom in the spring and cherries later in the year.

Did you know?

The fruit of the Cherry plum tree is a key ingredient in Georgian cuisine.
