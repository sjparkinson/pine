Rowan

Sorbus aucuparia

Bright red berries (birds love them!). Compound leaf structure with a terminal leaflet at the end. The leaflets are long and serrated. Smooth, grey bark. Hairy, purple leaf buds. White flowers.

Also known as Mountain Ash because they grow well at altitude.
