Almond

Prunus dulcis

Serrated leaves, oval shaped and pointed.Some varieties of almond have long thin leaves that look very different though. White/pink blossom. The almonds grow within a green-grey, leathery, slightly furry hull.Â 

Almonds originate in Iran and are mentioned several times in the bible. The greek word for almond, amygdale, is where the word amygdalus comes from.
