Sweetgum

Liquidambar styraciflua

Star-like leaves with 5 points, a bit like a maple leaf but the leaves are single not in pairs on the stem. The flowers are green and covered in rusty hairs. The fruits are hard spiky balls (gum balls) containing two seeds each.
