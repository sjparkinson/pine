Himalayan Birch

Betula utilis

Bright white bark, papery and peeling in places. Himalayan Birches are even whiter than Silver Birches. The saplings look a bit like fake indoor trees because of their uniformly white bark!

Did you know?

Himalayan Birch Bark was used as paper for the writing of ancient Sanskrit scriptures.
