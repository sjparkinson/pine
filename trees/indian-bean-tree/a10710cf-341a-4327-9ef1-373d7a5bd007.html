Indian Bean Tree

Catalpa bignonioides

These trees are very striking and seem to grow very well in the rainy, cold British weather despite their origins in warmer climates, so they are a popular ornamental tree. Their leaves are huge, growing up to about 20cm wide, heartshaped and a beautiful pale green colour. Long brown beans dangle off the branches. The bean pods are narrow and can reach 40cm long.
